On-Call Rotation

Primary incident duty rotates among maintainers every seven days to spread operational load and reduce responder fatigue. No single maintainer should own the pager continuously for more than one week.

Emergency Rollback Authority

During an active outage, only the incident commander or a delegated release lead may authorize reverting a deployment. The team should restore service before building a complete root-cause timeline.

Scheduled Restart Checklist

Planned maintenance restarts require a checklist review by the operations coordinator. These routine restarts are separate from emergency rollback decisions during outages.

Post-Incident Documentation

After the service is stable, responders document customer impact, recovery timing, and follow-up actions in the incident report. Writing the report should not delay immediate restoration work.
