Travel Reimbursement

Employees can expense airfare, lodging, and ground transportation for approved business travel. Meal reimbursement requires itemized receipts, and alcohol is not reimbursable.

Remote Work Equipment

Staff members may request a monitor, keyboard, webcam, or headset for home office use. Equipment requests need manager approval before purchase and must be recorded in the asset inventory system.

Procurement Review

Software purchases above the department threshold require a procurement review. Requests that process customer data also need a security assessment before a contract is signed.
